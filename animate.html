Select a Faction

There are 4 factions to pick from above. You may click on the icons above to show an animated icon, as well as more information on the cultures of these factions, a bit of history, and their values.

Sylven Republic

The Sylvan Republic is a land of lush forests, rivers, and populated by all kinds of fey. Their culture is built upon their reverence to nature, as well as their power. Their religious devotion to nature has granted the Sylvan Republic the powers of nature. The republic has many druids, shamans, and other powerful fey creatures leading its armies, all of which revere the forest and live simple lives.

Most of the lands inside the Sylvan Republic is an untamed wilderness, civilization has barely touched these lands, though there are a few small settlements, as most of the population are fey, the need for settlements is minimal. With this fact in hand, these lands prove a difficult place to conquer, as the fey use their powers of illusions and nature to keep potential threats at bay. Though the fey can call upon the more dangerous forces of nature when the situation grows dire. Such as summoning powerful beasts, or even natural disasters.

The Arcanum Technocracy

The Arcanum is a land of wizards and spell crafters, who revere the pursuit of knowledge in all its forms. Their lands are dotted with universities and libraries, the land is dotted in many towers reaching high into the skies. The wizards of the Arcanum wield the raw magical energy of the world, as well as the powers of alchemy. They forgo the heavy machinery of the Imperium for alchemical enchantments that afford the same purposes. The wizards of the Arcanum are not to be trifled with.

The Arcanum’s real power is brought by harnessing the power of other dimensions. Their lands are home to a rift, a place where the energies of other dimensions seep in, along with these other realm’s powerful magical creatures. Harnessing these energies have given the Arcanum great power, and their alchemists have mastered distillation of this raw magical energy.

The Imperium

The Imperium is a powerful war machine, built on powerful machines and technology. Their lands are dotted by soot spewing machines and factories fueling their imperial war machine. But with their powerful technology, they wield powerful weapons, such as machine guns, tanks, and airplanes, but they also have towering walkers lumbering on the battlefield, some look more like ships on land. The Imperium is a military state, with many soldiers trained with unresolved discipline.

The Imperium’s war machine is matched only by its cunning and ambitious ruler. The Imperium looks to rid the lands of the dangerous orc clans once and for all, as well as ending the undead menace. But doing so requires time and calculated skirmishes, to weaken the clans. Though this is a long and tedious process, as a full-scale war would not end well, as it is obvious that once the Imperium has claimed the Black Steel Clans in the south, they will move on to try and claim the rest of the lands.

The Black Steel Clans

The Black Steel Clans are a land of barbaric clans of Orcs, goblins, and the undead. The nomadic tribes constantly wander their harsh and tainted lands, seeking food from their volcanic lands. While most of the time, the clans are more occupied with fighting amongst themselves, every so often, a powerful warlord is able to unite the land under his banner, and during these times the other nations of the world must combat the hordes of goblins, orcs, and undead. The Black Steel Clans are not just lumbering hordes, but also wield the dark magic of necromancy and witchcraft. The Orc shamans wield this terrible magic against their enemies.

The Black Steel Clans have been the scourge of the land. Every few decades, their true master emerges, a powerful lich. Many wars have been fought to end the lich, but so far no attempts have succeeded. The lich leads the Orc clans, along with hordes of the undead to one day take everything over. It is the Lich’s dark presence that has tainted these lands, but it is also what gives this land strength. The beats here have adapted to the dark energies, and have evolved into monstrous creatures, ever hungering for life.
